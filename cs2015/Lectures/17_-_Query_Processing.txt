====== 17 - Query Processing ======
Created Tuesday November 8 2016

===== Relational Algebra: =====
	A symbolic formal way of describing relational operations
	Says how, as well as what (order is important)
	Can use re-write rules to simplify and optimise complex queries


==== Basic Operators: ====
	Selection: σ
	Projection: π
	Rename: ρ
