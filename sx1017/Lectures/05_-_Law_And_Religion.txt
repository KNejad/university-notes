====== 05 - Law And Religion ======
Created Wednesday 12 October 2016

The European convention of human rights (Article 9) does not explicitly prohibit a country from having laws for an established religion. Whereas the American constitution does


===== Free exersise of religion: =====
	Allowing an individual to follow whatever religion they want


===== Establishment of religion: =====
	An established religion in a government which is given more power than other religions


===== The structure of protection: =====
	Belief: Freedom to believe what you want
	Expression: Freedom to express your religion
	Action: Freedom to act on your belief
